2.Kaayyoo (vision)

Kayyoon wanta at itti uumamteef qofa osoo hin taane wanta at itti taateefidha.

Wanta at itti taateef jechuun wanta taate kan at taatee uumamteef kaayyoo guddaa waaqayyo waliin si uume sana ta'uuf akka humna yookaan kahumsa sii tahuufi.
